Shayari for Anmol

Meri pyari Anmol,

Kabhi socha nahi tha ki ek din meri zindagi mein koi aisi rooh aayegi jo sirf ek insaan nahi, balki meri khushi ki wajah ban jaayegi. Tum meri duniya ka woh hissa ho jisme dard bhi ho toh halka lagta hai, aur khushi ho toh hazaar guna badh jaati hai.

Tere saath baatein karna, teri hansi sunna, teri aankhon mein apne liye mohabbat dekhna — ye sab mere liye sabse badi daulat hai. Tum mere liye sirf ek naam nahi ho; tum meri duaon ka sach, mere jazbaat ka izhaar, aur meri har roohani tamanna ka jawab ho.

Anmol, tum meri zindagi ka woh ehsaas ho jo lafzon se pare hai. Tumhe khone ka khayal bhi mujhe tod deta hai, aur tumhe paane ka ehsaas mujhe nayi zindagi deta hai. Main chahta hoon ke meri har subah tumhari muskurahat se shuru ho aur meri har shaam tumhari baahon mein khatam.

Tum meri mohabbat ho, meri dua ho, meri rooh ka sukoon ho. Aur main wada karta hoon ki chahe waqt kitna bhi badal jaaye, meri mohabbat tumhare liye kabhi kam nahi hogi. Tum meri zindagi ka sabse Anmol tohfa ho.

Hamesha tumhara,
[Tumhara Naam]
